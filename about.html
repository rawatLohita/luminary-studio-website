Who we are

Design with purpose, built to last

Luminary Studio was founded in 2013 with a simple belief: great design solves real problems. We're a team of strategists, designers, and engineers united by a love of craft and an obsession with quality.

We work with startups finding their footing and established companies reinventing themselves — always as true partners, invested in outcomes that matter. Over 120 projects later, our process remains the same: listen deeply, think carefully, and execute with precision.
